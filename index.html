About Us

The Red Cross Society at the University of Ilorin is a community of dedicated students committed to making a difference. We focus on promoting health, providing first aid training, and supporting humanitarian efforts both on campus and beyond.
Through volunteering, outreach programs, and emergency response activities, we strive to serve humanity and help those in need. Our goal is to build a generation of compassionate individuals who are ready to take action and create positive impact in society.
