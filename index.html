About Us

The Red Cross Society at the University of Ilorin is a group of passionate and service-driven students committed to making a meaningful impact in the community. As part of the wider Red Cross movement, we focus on promoting humanity, health, and safety through various activities both on campus and beyond.
Our members are actively involved in first aid training, health awareness campaigns, blood donation drives, and community outreach programs. We work together to respond to emergencies, support vulnerable individuals, and educate others on life-saving skills that can make a difference in critical situations.
